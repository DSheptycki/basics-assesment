Omelette in the movie.

After Linguine was told to take the rat they found in the store far away and kill it, while about to drop him into a stream the rat seemed to react like a human and with human emotion. So Linguine brought it back to his house and gave it a comfortable place to sleep because he needed to remake the soup for his boss in the morning. Before Linguine goes to work Remy scavenged some ingredients to make him and Linguine a nice breakfast omelette.






Ingredients

  • 6 large egg whites
  • 5 large eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon white pepper
  • Gluten free nonstick cooking spray
  • ¼ cup fat free feta cheese
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh chives
  • 2 cups ratatouille


Instructions

  • Whisk egg whites, eggs, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Makes about 2 cups.
  • Heat ratatouille in a small saucepan over medium heat 3 minutes or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
  • Heat a small nonstick skillet over medium heat; spray with nonstick cooking spray. Add ½ cup egg mixture; cook 1 minute or until eggs begin to set. Lift edges of omelet with rubber spatula as it cooks, allowing uncooked eggs to run underneath; cook 2 minutes or until eggs are set.
  • Add ½ cup ratatouille and 1 tablespoon cheese down center of omelet; fold 1 side of omelet towards center over ratatouille, then tilt pan to roll omelet, seam side down, onto plate. Repeat 3 times with nonstick cooking spray, and remaining egg mixture, ratatouille and cheese to make 3 additional omelets. Makes 4 omelets.
